Meeting Revenue Targets — The Operating Rules
Weekly
VP Alliances delivers revenue forecast every Friday 5pm without CEO asking. ±10% accuracy required. Any deal at risk flagged proactively. No surprises at month-end.
Monthly
Pipeline coverage ≥3× monthly target maintained at all times. If coverage drops below 2×, VP Alliances triggers emergency AE activation campaign. Head of GTM delivers attribution report within 3 days of month close.
Quarterly
5 partner QBRs complete before Month 1 of new quarter. Referral targets set per partner. MDF fully allocated. Win/loss analysis complete within 10 days. ICP updated. GTM strategy review CEO-attended once per year.
Deal Escalation Rules
$25K+ → VP Alliances briefed same day. $50K+ → CEO joins discovery call. CEO signs all SOWs above $50K. No exceptions. One below-minimum deal signals the floor is negotiable — it never is.

Lead Generation → Closed Deal: End-to-End Flow
1. Lead In
Partner intro OR SDR reply OR inbound form. Logged in CRM same day. Source tagged.
Owner: Alliance Mgr/AE
2. Qualify
BANT qual in ≤24hr. Is this ICP? Does pain match? Budget? Timeline? Stakeholder access?
Owner: Alliance Mgr/AE
3. Discovery
45-min discovery call. Map pain to DataSkate solution. Involve Delivery Head for complex scope. VP if $25K+.
Owner: Alliance Mgr/AE + VP
4. Scope & SOW
VP + Delivery Head scope. SOW in PandaDoc within 48hr. Floor enforced. Register deal same day.
Owner: VP Alliances
5. Close
Follow up in 48hr. Handle objections. If 5 days no signature → VP escalates. CEO involved if $50K+.
Owner: Alliance Mgr/AE + VP
6. Handoff
Signed SOW → Delivery Head briefed same day. Kickoff scheduled within 5 days. Partner AE notified of win.
Owner: VP Alliances → Delivery Head
