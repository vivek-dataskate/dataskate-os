🚀 90-day onboarding ramp — VP Alliances
Ramp context
The VP Alliances role is the revenue cornerstone of DataSkate. The first 90 days have a single gate: is pipeline building and are partners referring? All activity should answer that question. CEO receives a 30-day check-in and a 90-day formal ramp review.
Days 1–30 · Learn the ecosystem
Meet all 5 partner PDMs — intro call, understand their current DataSkate awareness
Shadow 3 active deals from CRM — understand DataSkate's deal motion
Read all P1 process docs — P01 to P21 (revenue category)
Learn all 5 partner portals, deal reg process, MDF eligibility
Map AE landscape — who are the 10 highest-potential AEs per partner?
30-day CEO gate: partner intros done, AE map submitted
Days 31–60 · Build the pipeline
First deal registration live in at least 2 partner portals
MDF claim submitted for any eligible co-marketing spend
First Friday CEO revenue report delivered on time — no chasing
Weekly pipeline cadence with Alliance Mgr established and running
First co-sell motion running with at least 1 partner AE
60-day gate: pipeline 1× target, first GTM brief to Head of GTM sent
Days 61–90 · Run at full pace
Pipeline at 2× quarterly target — enough to forecast confidently
All 5 partner relationships have active touchpoints this month
First QBR prep initiated with all partners
SOW floor being enforced — zero sub-minimum deals in pipeline
Cross-pillar cadence running: weekly brief to Head of GTM, monthly to DH
90-day gate: partner NPS surveyed, forecast accuracy within ±15%
