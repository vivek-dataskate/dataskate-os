🚀 90-day onboarding ramp — Alliance Manager / AE
Ramp context
The Alliance Mgr / AE is DataSkate's deal executor. Every referral that doesn't move to SOW is a wasted partner relationship. The 90-day ramp proves one thing: can you move a lead to a signed contract reliably and without VP hand-holding? The first deal closed is the gate.
Days 1–30 · Learn the deals
Shadow 2–3 active deals in HubSpot from beginning to end
Read all P1 revenue process docs: P01–P21
Learn all 5 partner portals: deal reg, MDF, co-sell tools
Set up CRM, sequences, Calendly, Zoom correctly
Meet 5 partner AEs via intro from VP Alliances
30-day gate: CRM set up, 5 AE intros done, 1 solo discovery call attempted
Days 31–60 · Run the motion
Own first live deal end-to-end (VP available to advise, not do)
Deal reg in at least 2 portals for active deals
Weekly pipeline cadence with VP established and running
3 proactive partner AE touches per week — documented in CRM
First SOW draft submitted and reviewed by VP
60-day gate: 1 SOW in negotiation or signed, CRM hygiene score reviewed
Days 61–90 · Prove the machine
First deal closed (any size) — demonstrates full motion capability
AE map built: 10+ active partner AEs tracked in CRM
All cross-pillar handoffs running correctly — DH and Finance briefed on new deals
Win/loss first debrief submitted to Head of GTM
90-day gate: 1 deal closed, AE map done, VP formally signs off on ramp
