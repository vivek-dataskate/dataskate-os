🚀 90-day onboarding ramp — SDR / BDR
Ramp context — SDR joins at Day 61 of the Head of GTM ramp
The SDR is the volume engine. They join after Head of GTM has proven the sequences and messaging work (Days 1–60). The SDR inherits validated sequences — they don't start from scratch. Head of GTM manages them daily. VP Alliances reviews KPIs weekly. First 30 days are learning + light ramp. Full KPIs apply from Month 3.
Days 1–30 · Learn the motion
Shadow Head of GTM running sequences — understand the full tool stack
Read the SDR/BDR Outbound Playbook end-to-end — takes 2 days
Set up Apollo, Clay, Instantly, Aircall, HubSpot accounts correctly
Run first sequences at 30% volume (5–8 prospects/day) with Head of GTM review
First cold call live by Day 7 with Head of GTM listening in
30-day gate: 1 booked meeting (no quality bar — just prove you can get one)
Days 31–60 · Build the habit
Scale to 70% volume (10–12 prospects/day)
Weekly KPI report started — first report to Head of GTM by end of Week 5
First prospect brief sent to AE on any booked meeting — quality reviewed by Head of GTM
First A/B test run on one sequence variant — results reviewed
60-day gate: 2 meetings/week minimum, reply rate ≥1.5%, all CRM logged
Days 61–90 · Hit full KPIs
Full volume: 12–15 prospects/day, 15–20 dials/day
4 meetings/week target from Day 75
Full sequence suite running — VP Alliances approves named account additions
First sequence rewrite ownership — SDR owns one sequence update solo
90-day gate: KPI floors hit for 2 consecutive weeks, VP and Head of GTM sign-off
