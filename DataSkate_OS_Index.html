Why DataSkate Exists

Read this before anything else. Understanding why we exist changes how you do your job. This is not a mission statement on a wall — this is the operating context every decision you make should sit inside.

The Founder's Vision
We exist to make
integration simple
for businesses that can't afford to wait.
Salesforce, MuleSoft, Workato, Boomi, Informatica — these are transformational platforms. But most companies that buy them can't get full value without expert help. The enterprise SIs charge $500K+ and take 18 months. Freelancers are cheap but unreliable. DataSkate sits in the middle: specialist, fast, quality, and partner-first. We deliver in weeks, not quarters. We build it right the first time. And we use our partner relationships to generate business rather than wait for it.

The Problem We Solve
Companies buy platforms and can't use them
Every year, thousands of companies buy Salesforce, MuleSoft, Workato, or Boomi licences and then struggle to deploy them. They're paying for software they can't use. Their internal teams don't have the specialist skills. The big SIs are too slow and too expensive. Nothing happens. This is our market.
⚡
How We Solve It
Fast, specialist delivery. Partner-powered. Quality-gated.
DataSkate delivers integration projects in 4 weeks for SMB and 8–12 weeks for mid-market. We use Certified specialists, not generalists. Every project has architecture review, code standards, and a CSAT gate. We source 60%+ of our deals through partner AE relationships — the same partners who sold the client the licence in the first place. We make the partner look good. That's the flywheel.

The Market Opportunity
$30M valuation by December 2028
The Salesforce ecosystem alone generates $6 for every $1 of Salesforce revenue in partner services. MuleSoft, Workato, and Boomi add more. DataSkate's addressable market is every SMB and mid-market company in APAC, South Asia, and MEA that has bought one of our 5 partner platforms and needs help using it. That is hundreds of thousands of companies. We are going after a focused slice of them — and doing it better than anyone else in our tier.
Why DataSkate Wins — and Why We Don't Compete on Every Deal
We win when the client needs
Fast delivery (4–12 weeks), specialist expertise across 5 platforms, a trusted SI that the partner AE personally vouches for, and a structured process they can audit. These clients value reliability over cheapness and speed over enterprise prestige.
We lose when the client needs
A brand-name global SI for a board-level decision, a single-platform specialist (we do 5), or a project under $5K where our process overhead doesn't make commercial sense. We don't chase these. We qualify them out fast and don't look back.
Our unfair advantage
We generate pipeline FOR the partner AE before asking for a referral. No other boutique SI does this systematically. When a Salesforce AE compares DataSkate to our competitors, we're the only one who brought them leads. That relationship is not transactional — it compounds. Every AE we help becomes a referral machine.
What makes us defensible
Multi-platform (5 partners, not 1). Fresher ramp model (low-cost capacity with quality gates). Partner co-sell motion at scale. CSAT-first culture — every project creates a reference. IP library that makes the next delivery faster. None of these are easy to copy quickly.
How We Operate — 4 Non-Negotiable Principles
Partner first, always
Every partner AE interaction is an investment, not a transaction. We make them look good before we ask for anything. If a partner AE looks bad because of DataSkate, we have failed — regardless of whether the project technically delivered.
Quality is not negotiable
CSAT ≥4.2 is the floor, not the target. Every go-live requires DH sign-off. No code goes to production without code review. If we ship a bad project, we lose the reference, the repeat business, and the partner AE's trust. One bad delivery costs more than the deal was worth.
Speed without shortcuts
We deliver in 4 weeks for SMB because we are structured and fast — not because we cut corners. The CoE exists to make fast delivery safe. Accelerators, templates, and standards mean we can move quickly without reinventing the wheel or skipping architecture review.
Escalate early, own the outcome
We surface problems early. A risk flagged on Day 5 is manageable. A risk hidden until Day 25 is a crisis. Every role at DataSkate is expected to raise blockers the same day they appear — not to solve them alone for a week and then panic. Escalation is not failure. Silence is.
