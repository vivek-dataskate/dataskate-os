📤 What you send up — CEO briefing format and obligations
Friday 3pm CEO Briefing — fixed 5-section format · max 1 page · one ask
Sent every Friday by 3pm without exception. CEO reads in under 3 minutes. Admin Head acts on Amber before sending. CEO only sees Red requires action.
1. HEADCOUNT
Hired / leaving / open roles / risk signal. RAG. Example: GREEN 2 roles in process, on track. OR RED Senior Dev Lead resigned — P10 (P2) KT activated, replacement H01 filed.
2. FINANCE
Invoices issued / collected / overdue / DSO / cash. RAG. Example: AMBER Client X invoice Day 22 — Admin Head chasing. No CEO action yet. OR RED DSO 38 days — CEO call to Client Y exec requested.
3. COMPLIANCE
Open legal items / breach flags / audit triggers. RAG. Example: GREEN All MSAs signed, DPAs current. OR AMBER Partner NDA renewal due in 30 days — Admin Head coordinating.
4. ENABLEMENT
Ramps on track / certs in flight / passes this week. RAG. Example: GREEN 2 ramps on track. 1 cert passed (Priya, Salesforce Admin). OR RED D91 gate failure — [Name] not billable. DH notified. Margin impact flagged.
5. FLAGS & ASKS
Max 1 CEO decision needed. Everything else Admin Head resolves. Example: "Need CEO approval on salary band increase for Dev Lead hire — band is 12% above current max."
P0 Escalation — bypasses Friday briefing entirely
Data breach detectedAdmin Head calls CEO within 1 hour. Not Slack. Phone call.
Senior resignation (Practice Head or above)Admin Head notifies CEO same hour. CEO decides on counter-offer.
Regulator contact or legal threatAdmin Head notifies CEO and retains counsel same day.
Any event where 7-day delay causes material harmAdmin Head judgement call. When in doubt — call CEO.
