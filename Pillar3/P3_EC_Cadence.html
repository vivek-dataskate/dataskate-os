Flags & asks
Anything needing Admin Head or CEO attention. If nothing — "No flags."
If nothing to report — send "All ramps on track. No certs due this week. No flags." Still send it. Friday 12pm. Every week.
Red flags that bypass Friday — D91 gate failure (email Admin Head same day) · Cert pass rate drops below 60% (flag immediately) · Ramp completely blocked (flag immediately, do not wait).
