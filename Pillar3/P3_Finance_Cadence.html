Finance Lead Must NOT Do These
✕
Chase PM for invoice triggers — the system alerts. Finance escalates to Admin Head if PM misses SLA.
✕
Issue invoices without PM milestone confirmation — issuing against unconfirmed milestones is a compliance and relationship risk.
✕
Contact client directly after Day 30 — Admin Head takes over collections at Day 30. Finance supports.
✕
Approve own expenses — Admin Head approves Finance Lead expenses. Segregation of duties.
✕
Make tool purchase decisions — Admin Head approves all tools. Finance pays and tracks.
✕
Release payroll without Admin Head approval — D-3 lock and review is mandatory before any payroll run.
