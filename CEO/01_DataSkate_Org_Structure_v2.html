Pillar 1 · Revenue
Partner Alliance
& GTM
Head: VP of Alliances
3–4 people · primary revenue engine
Roles
VP of Alliances
Exec
Owns: all five partner relationships (Salesforce, MuleSoft, Informatica, Workato, Boomi), partner tier strategy, QBR cadence, MDF utilisation, AppExchange listing, deal registration, SOW pricing authority up to $50K.
KPI: ≥60% of new ARR from partner referral · partner NPS ≥4/5
Alliance Manager / AE
Lead
Owns: day-to-day AE relationships with partner field teams, co-sell call participation, deal qualification, SOW scoping, inbound lead follow-up within 24 hours, deal registration in partner portals.
KPI: pipeline coverage 3x target · SOW min $5K SMB / $25K MM enforced
GTM & Marketing (inside this pillar)
Head of GTM
Lead
Owns: full GTM engine — inbound AND outbound. Content, personal branding (CEO/VP/DH), collateral, AppExchange, MDF, case studies, events. PLUS: outbound strategy (ICP, sequence library, Apollo/Clay/Instantly), SDR/BDR management, weekly KPI reporting to VP Alliances. Sets strategy independently — does NOT take brief passively. Reports to VP Alliances. Manages SDR/BDR as direct report.
KPI: Outbound pipeline ≥30% of qualified leads · SDR 4–6 calls/week at ramp · CEO LinkedIn 5K followers EOY · Weekly KPI report every Friday
SDR / BDR
Outbound
Owns: prospect list building (Apollo/Clay), email sequences (Instantly), LinkedIn DMs, cold calls, qualifying calls (5-question, <10 min), 3-way call booking (prospect + DataSkate + partner AE), CRM logging. Reports to Head of GTM — not VP Alliances. Joins 6–8 weeks after Head of GTM into a working system.
KPI: 60 prospects/week · 15–20 dials/day · 4–6 booked 3-way calls/week at ramp · bounce rate <3%
RevOps Function
Function
Note: RevOps is a function, not a dedicated headcount early stage. Owned by VP Alliances with support from Admin. Responsibilities: CRM hygiene (Salesforce/HubSpot), pipeline reporting, SOW minimum enforcement, revenue forecast to CEO weekly.
KPI: CRM updated weekly · forecast accuracy ±10%
Pillar 2 · Delivery & CoE
Delivery
Head: Delivery Head / SA
Largest team · all billable except Delivery Head + PM + Practice Heads
Leadership Roles
Delivery Head / Solution Architect
Exec · CoE
Owns: all delivery quality and standards across every practice. Solution architecture on strategic accounts. Architecture Review Board (ARB) gate authority. Coding standards definition. Reusable accelerators and IP library. Practice capability strategy (the kind of document you uploaded). Cert roadmap per role. Reports directly to CEO. Hire first — everything else is blocked until this seat is filled.
KPI: CSAT ≥4.2 · 0 go-lives without ARB sign-off · accelerator library grows each quarter
Project Manager (shared)
Lead
Owns: timelines, client status reporting, risk log, escalation management, invoicing triggers. Runs 3–4 projects simultaneously across all practices. Does NOT make technical decisions or manage team performance. Add a second PM when concurrent projects exceed 8.
KPI: on-time delivery ≥85% · client status sent weekly without chasing
Practice Heads (non-billable · own practice P&L)
Practice Head — Salesforce / Agentforce
Head
Owns: full practice P&L, delivery standards, and team performance for Salesforce and Agentforce. Manages Dev Leads within this practice. Defines hiring brief for Dev Leads. Technical scoping on discovery calls alongside VP Alliances. Does NOT write production code. Reports to Delivery Head.
KPI: practice CSAT ≥4.2 · 0 ARB violations · Dev Lead pods at ≥75% utilisation
Practice Head — MuleSoft
Head
Owns: protecting and growing the $1M ARR MuleSoft base. Account health across all MuleSoft clients. Upsell path to Salesforce Data Cloud and Informatica. Manages Dev Leads within the MuleSoft practice. Sets Anypoint Platform delivery standards.
KPI: MuleSoft ARR growth ≥20% YoY · account churn 0% · Dev Lead pods utilised ≥75%
Practice Head — Informatica / IDMC
Head
Owns: IDMC, MDM, data quality practice standards. Cross-sell motion from existing MuleSoft accounts. Manages Dev Leads within this practice. Integration data stories for Salesforce Data Cloud. Start this quarter.
KPI: 3 new SOWs in first 6 months · 1 certified architect on team
Practice Head — Workato / Boomi
Head
Owns: both iPaaS practices (shared head early stage — skill overlap is high). Workato for SMB fast-cycle deals. Boomi for mid-market. Manages Dev Leads across both tools. Split into two heads when each practice exceeds 3 concurrent Dev Lead pods.
KPI: 5 SMB SOWs in first 6 months · avg cycle ≤6 weeks
Dev Leads & Pod Structure (billable · player-coaches)
Dev Lead (e.g. Sasank)
Lead
Owns: a pod of 5 (1 Developer + 3 Freshers). Manages 2–3 SMB projects simultaneously. Sprint planning, daily standups, client technical communication, 100% PR code review within pod. Billable ≥70%. Reports to Practice Head. Does NOT own partner relationships or SOW pricing.
KPI: billability ≥70% · CSAT ≥4.2 · on-time ≥85% · intern billable by day 91
Developer Ladder (uniform across all practices)
Career progression
Fresher / Jr Dev → Developer → Dev Lead → Practice Head → Delivery Head / SA
Developer
Mid
Owns: feature development within defined scope. Unit testing. Documentation. Billable from day one. Works within standards defined by Delivery Head and enforced by Dev Lead. Supervises Freshers within the pod day-to-day.
1–3 yrs exp · billable ≥80% · pursuing next cert · 1 per pod
Fresher / Junior Developer
Junior
Owns: learning. Follows the 90-day ramp plan designed by Delivery Head and administered by Admin. Configuration tasks, documentation, testing support. Supervised by Developer, reviewed by Dev Lead. Billable at reduced rate from day 31. Full billable from day 91.
3 per pod · billable by day 91 · improves DS margins

Pillar 3 · Internal
Administration
Head: Admin Head / COO
2–3 people · internal-facing only
Leadership Role
Admin Head / COO
Exec
Owns: all internal operations — HR, finance, legal, tools, enablement administration. Coordinates part-time accountant and contract recruiter. Does NOT define hiring specs or training content (that comes from Delivery Head). Title can be Chief of Staff or Head of Operations early stage.
KPI: all roles filled per hiring plan · P&L reported monthly · zero contract lapses
HR & Talent Acquisition
HR Manager / Talent Lead
Lead
Owns: recruiting execution (not spec — spec comes from Delivery Head), onboarding logistics, offer letters, employment contracts, performance review process administration, comp bands (defined with CEO and CFO), retention planning. Manages relationship with contract recruiter for volume hiring.
KPI: time-to-hire ≤45 days · offer acceptance rate ≥80% · attrition ≤15%
Internal Enablement
Enablement Coordinator
Ops
Owns: administering the 90-day fresher ramp program (designed by Delivery Head). Scheduling internal training sessions. Tracking certification progress per employee. Managing LMS or learning tools. Coordinating cert exam bookings and reimbursements. This role does NOT design content — they deliver and track it.
KPI: 100% freshers on ramp plan · cert completion rate ≥80% · training calendar published monthly
Finance & Ops
Finance Lead / Accountant
Finance
Owns: monthly P&L, invoicing and collections, payroll coordination, budget tracking per pillar, headcount cost tracking, partner portal billing, vendor contract renewals. Can be a fractional CFO or part-time accountant at current stage.
KPI: invoices sent within 5 days of SOW milestone · DSO ≤30 days
Legal & Contracts (function)
Function
Owns: MSA and SOW review, NDA management, partner agreement compliance, IP protection. At current stage this is handled by a retained external lawyer — not a full-time hire. Admin Head coordinates and is the point of contact.
KPI: all SOWs legally reviewed before signature · zero unsigned MSAs
Tools & Systems Admin (function)
Function
Owns: Jira, CRM (Salesforce/HubSpot), Slack, Google Workspace, partner portals, LMS, time-tracking. Managed by Admin Head with input from VP Alliances (CRM) and Delivery Head (project tools). Tool decisions approved by CEO and Finance.
KPI: all tools provisioned on day 1 for new hires · tool cost per head reviewed quarterly
Critical handshake: Admin ↔ Delivery
Admin does not hire or train independently. Delivery Head defines the cert spec and role brief → Admin recruits to that spec → Delivery Head interviews for technical fit. Without this handshake, HR attracts the wrong candidates and enablement trains to the wrong standard.
