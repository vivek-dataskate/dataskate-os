01
Lead Received
A partner AE refers a prospect or an inbound enquiry comes in — the revenue motion starts here
⏱ 1–3 days
How leads arrive
  • SF
    Salesforce AE referral — highest value. AE intro-emails DS to a prospect they are co-selling with.
    Owner: Alliance Mgr/AE responds within 24hr
  • P
    Partner portal lead — MuleSoft, Informatica, Workato, Boomi CAMs pass leads via partner portal or email.
    Owner: Alliance Mgr/AE logs in CRM same day
  • IB
    Inbound (AppExchange, LinkedIn, website) — prospect finds DS independently. Lower volume at this stage.
    Owner: Alliance Mgr/AE responds within 24hr
  • CS
    Client referral — happy client refers another company. Highest close rate of all lead types.
    Owner: VP Alliances tracks and credits source
Actions in this stage
  • 1
    Log lead in CRM within 24hr — source, contact, platform, rough size.
    Alliance Mgr/AE
  • 2
    Acknowledge prospect with a same-day response confirming receipt and next step.
    Alliance Mgr/AE
  • 3
    Notify VP Alliances — check if above $25K threshold (mid-market needs VP on first call).
    Alliance Mgr/AE
  • 4
    Register deal in partner portal immediately — do not wait until close.
    Alliance Mgr/AE

02
Discovery & Qualification
Determine if this is a real opportunity, the right size, and the right fit for DS's practices
⏱ 2–5 days
Qualification checklist (BANT)
  • B
    Budget — can they spend $5K+ (SMB) or $25K+ (MM)? Do they have budget approval or is it informal?
  • A
    Authority — who signs the SOW? Is the decision-maker in the room or do we need to get there?
  • N
    Need — is this a DS practice fit? (Salesforce, MuleSoft, Informatica, Workato, Boomi) Does it need our skills?
  • T
    Timeline — when do they want to start? Is there urgency or is this exploratory?
Discovery call agenda
  • 1
    Understand current state — what platform, what pain, what broke?
    Alliance Mgr/AE leads. Practice Lead joins for MM.
  • 2
    Define desired outcome — what does success look like in 90 days?
    Alliance Mgr/AE asks. Practice Lead validates feasibility.
  • 3
    Surface constraints — budget range, timeline, internal resources available.
    Alliance Mgr/AE
  • 4
    Confirm next step — agree a date for scoping call and SOW.
    Alliance Mgr/AE closes the call
Roles active
VP Alliances Alliance Mgr/AE Practice Lead (MM only)
Stage exit — qualify or disqualify
Qualify
BANT passes all four criteria — move to scoping
Nurture
Budget or timeline not confirmed — park and follow up in 30 days. Do not spend scoping time on unconfirmed budget.
Disqualify
Below SOW minimum, wrong platform, or no decision-maker access — log reason in CRM and close. Do not proceed.
Required
Update CRM with discovery notes and qualification status before moving forward
03
Scoping & SOW
Define exactly what will be built, by whom, in how long, and at what price — no ambiguity
⏱ SMB 3–7 days · Mid-Market 7–14 days
Scoping activities
  • S
    Technical scoping call — Practice Lead walks through current architecture, integration points, data volumes, user counts.
    Practice Lead leads · Delivery Head/SA joins for complex MM
  • A
    Solution approach defined — Delivery Head/SA signs off on the proposed architecture before SOW is written. This is the pre-ARB check.
    Delivery Head/SA
  • E
    Effort estimation — Practice Lead estimates story points / days. Sr Dev input for technical complexity.
    Practice Lead + Sr Dev
  • R
    Resource plan — which team members will be assigned. Confirm availability against current project load.
    Delivery Head/SA + PM
SOW components (mandatory)
✓Scope statement — what IS included in plain language
✓Out of scope — explicit list of what is NOT included
✓Deliverables — named outputs, not activities
✓Timeline — start date, milestone dates, go-live date
✓Fixed price or T&M — stated clearly with payment milestones
✓Assumptions — dependencies on client side (access, data, SMEs)
✓Change control clause — what happens if scope changes
!Min $5K SMB / $25K MM — RevOps enforces, VP Alliances approves
Roles active
VP Alliances Delivery Head/SA Practice Lead Project Mgr Finance Lead
Stage exit — SOW sign-off gates
Required
Delivery Head/SA approves solution approach before SOW is sent to client — no exceptions
Required
CEO approves SOWs above $50K before client signature
Required
Legal reviews MSA in place before SOW is countersigned
Required
Finance Lead adds to revenue forecast and invoicing schedule
Block
Do not start any delivery work before SOW is countersigned by client — even if client says "just get started"
04
Project Kick-off
Set the tone, align on process, and unblock all dependencies before a single line of code is written
⏱ 3–5 days
Kick-off meeting agenda
  • 1
    Introductions — client team meets DS team. PM, Practice Lead, Sr Dev introduced.
    PM facilitates
  • 2
    SOW walkthrough — review scope, out-of-scope, and assumptions with client. Confirm shared understanding.
    PM + Practice Lead
  • 3
    Communication cadence agreed — weekly status call day/time, Slack channel or email, escalation path.
    PM locks in and sends calendar invites
  • 4
    Access provisioning checklist handed to client — Salesforce org, MuleSoft env, data files, SME availability.
    Practice Lead defines · PM chases
  • 5
    Sprint 1 backlog confirmed — first two weeks of work defined, story points agreed.
    Practice Lead + Sr Dev
ARB gate (Architecture Review Board)
  • A
    Delivery Head/SA runs the ARB — formal review of solution design before build begins. Internal DS meeting, not client-facing.
    Delivery Head/SA chairs · Practice Lead presents
  • R
    Reference architecture reviewed — does the proposed design follow DS standards? Naming conventions, error handling, logging, security.
    Delivery Head/SA + Practice Lead + Sr Dev
  • O
    Output: ARB sign-off document — written approval or list of changes required before build can start.
    Delivery Head/SA signs off
Roles active
Delivery Head/SA Practice Lead Project Mgr Sr Dev
Stage exit — before build starts
Required
ARB signed off by Delivery Head/SA — solution design approved
Required
All access provisioned — dev org, integration endpoints, test data available
Required
Jira board set up with Sprint 1 backlog, story points, assignees
Required
Communication cadence confirmed and calendar invites sent
Block
No access = no build. PM escalates to client sponsor if access not received within 3 business days of kick-off
05
Delivery (Build Sprints)
The execution engine — structured, standards-governed, and transparent to the client at all times
⏱ SMB 4–8 weeks · Mid-Market 8–16 weeks
Sprint rhythm (every 2 weeks)
  • M
    Monday — Sprint planning: Practice Lead breaks backlog into tasks. Sr Dev estimates. Freshers assigned to low-risk items.
    Practice Lead + Sr Dev
  • D
    Daily — Standup (15 min): what did I do, what will I do, any blockers. PM listens. Blockers escalated same day.
    Practice Lead runs · PM logs blockers
  • PR
    Continuous — PR review: every pull request reviewed by Sr Dev before merge. No direct commits to main branch.
    Sr Dev mandatory · Practice Lead for complex PRs
  • W
    Weekly — Client status call: PM presents progress, risks, next steps. 30 min max. Status report sent same day.
    PM facilitates · Practice Lead answers technical Qs
  • E
    End of sprint — Demo + review: show working functionality to client. Capture feedback. Adjust next sprint if needed.
    Practice Lead demos · PM manages client expectations
Quality standards — non-negotiable
  • C
    Coding standards: DS naming conventions, error handling patterns, and logging standards applied on every file.
    Defined by Delivery Head/SA · enforced by Practice Lead
  • V
    Version control: Git branching strategy — feature branches, PR to dev, promote to staging, never direct to prod.
    Practice Lead sets up · Sr Dev enforces
  • T
    Unit testing: Developer writes unit tests for every component built. Minimum coverage defined by Delivery Head/SA per practice.
    Dev/Fresher writes · Sr Dev reviews
  • D
    Documentation: every flow, API, and integration documented as built — not retrospectively.
    Dev/Fresher documents · Practice Lead reviews
Escalation paths
L1Technical blocker → Practice Lead resolves same day
L2Scope creep request → PM logs, VP Alliances prices change, CEO approves if >20% uplift
L3Client unhappy with delivery → Delivery Head/SA joins next call within 24hr
L4Project at risk → CEO informed, VP Alliances manages partner relationship
Delivery stage gates
Per PR
Code review by Sr Dev — no code merges without review. Practice Lead for high-risk changes.
Per Sprint
Sprint demo to client — working functionality shown, feedback captured, Jira updated
Weekly
Status report sent to client by PM — RAG status, progress %, risks, next steps
Mid-project
Delivery Head/SA checkpoint on projects over 8 weeks — architectural drift check at midpoint
Block
No promotion to staging or UAT environment without Practice Lead sign-off that sprint is complete
Roles active in delivery
Delivery Head/SA Practice Lead Project Mgr Sr Dev Dev / Fresher
06
QA, UAT & Go-Live
Three formal checkpoints before anything touches production — no exceptions
⏱ 1 week
QA phase (DS internal)
  • 1
    Test plan executed against all scope items. Practice Lead signs test coverage is complete.
    Practice Lead + Sr Dev
  • 2
    Defect triage — severity 1 and 2 defects fixed before UAT. Severity 3 logged for post-go-live.
    Practice Lead triages · Dev/Fresher fixes
  • 3
    Regression testing — confirm existing functionality not broken by new changes.
    Sr Dev + Fresher (good use of fresher time)
  • 4
    Delivery Head/SA reviews final build against ARB-approved architecture. Final internal sign-off.
    Delivery Head/SA
UAT (client-side)
  • U
    UAT environment set up and access given to client's SMEs. PM manages client schedule.
    PM co-ordinates · Practice Lead supports
  • S
    UAT scripts provided to client — step-by-step test cases matched to agreed scope.
    Practice Lead writes scripts
  • F
    UAT feedback consolidated by PM. In-scope bugs fixed within 48hr. Out-of-scope items logged as change requests.
    PM manages · Practice Lead fixes
  • ✓
    Client UAT sign-off — written confirmation (email or form) that UAT is complete and client approves go-live.
    PM collects · VP Alliances notified
Roles active
Delivery Head/SA Practice Lead Project Mgr Sr Dev Dev / Fresher
Go-live gates — all must pass
Required
Delivery Head/SA internal sign-off — architecture matches ARB approval, standards met
Required
Zero Sev-1 or Sev-2 defects open at time of go-live
Required
Client UAT sign-off received in writing — email acceptable
Required
Rollback plan documented — if go-live fails, what is the recovery path and who executes?
Required
Finance milestone triggered — PM notifies Finance Lead to issue milestone invoice on go-live day
Block
No go-live without all five above. If client pressures to skip — VP Alliances manages the conversation, not the PM.
07
Hypercare, CSAT & Expansion
The project ends but the client relationship does not — this stage determines repeat business and referrals
⏱ 2 weeks hypercare + ongoing relationship
2-week hypercare period
  • H
    Practice Lead and Sr Dev remain available for production issues. SLA: Sev-1 response within 2hr, Sev-2 within 8hr.
    Practice Lead + Sr Dev on standby
  • C
    Daily check-in with client for first 5 business days — short call or message to confirm system is stable.
    PM
  • D
    Final documentation handover — architecture docs, runbook, admin guide delivered to client.
    Practice Lead + Sr Dev finalize
  • T
    Knowledge transfer session — train client admin / super-users if not already done during UAT.
    Practice Lead runs · Sr Dev supports
CSAT, retro & expansion
  • S
    CSAT survey sent within 72hr of go-live — 5 questions max. Target ≥4.2/5. PM sends, VP Alliances reviews result.
    PM sends · Delivery Head/SA action plan if <4
  • R
    Internal retrospective — what went well, what did not, what changes to process or standards. Feeds back into CoE.
    PM facilitates · Delivery Head/SA updates CoE playbook
  • W
    Win logged to partner — VP Alliances updates deal in partner portal. Case study briefing started.
    VP Alliances + GTM Coordinator
  • $
    Expansion conversation — VP Alliances sets a 30-day check-in call to discuss phase 2, managed services, or adjacent practice upsell.
    VP Alliances leads · PM introduces
